ProMaIDes GIS Tools (version 0.7.1)

is a set of tools to support the working process with the software ProMaIDes. Model set-up and result visualisation gets very simple and straightforward. Have fun!

ProMaIDes

is a decision support system for flood risk analysis and the evaluation of flood risk mitigation measures in riverine and coastal area. It is developed by the research group Flood Risk Management (AG FRM) (University of applied sciences Magdeburg-Stendal) and the Institute of Hydraulic Engineering at RWTH Aachen University (IWW). The freeware software package provides a comprehensive set of modules:

  • 1D/2D hydraulic modeling
  • Analysis of consequences for economic damages, affected and endangered persons, ecological consequences and critical infrastructure
  • Monte-Carlo based dike and dune fragility analysis
  • Integrated flood risk analysis on catchment scale
  • MADM analysis

Further information about ProMaIDes and the plug-ins is provided here.

LoFloDes

is a decision support system for low flow risk analysis and the evaluation of low flow risk mitigation measures in riverine area. It is developed by University of applied sciences Magdeburg-Stendal and the Institute of Hydraulic Engineering at RWTH Aachen University (IWW). The freeware software package provides a comprehensive set of modules:

  • 1D/Groundwater hydraulic modeling
  • Analysis of consequences for economic damages of low flow
  • Integrated low flow risk analysis on catchment scale

Further information about LoFloDes and the plug-ins is provided here.
